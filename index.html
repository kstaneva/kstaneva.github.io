Multi Factor Authentication

We’re adding Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) via email to make your Okta account more secure. This means you’ll receive a verification email to confirm your identity when logging in. You will receive the verification on the same email address that you use to log in.

Customers will notice the new log on process on 1st of May 2025. Until then, you should log on as you currently do.

We’re adding this extra security step to protect your account and personal data. It helps prevent unauthorized access by requiring more than just your password to log in. Logging in will take only a few more seconds than it does at the moment, but the process will be much more secure.

You will log onto your account as usual by providing username and password. Then a prompt will appear indicating that verification is required. Once you click on the button to prove, you will receive an email with a magic link and a code that will be used for your identity verification.

Unfortunately, it is not possible to enable a different multi-factor authentication (MFA) method at the moment.

If you share an email login with others, you might face challenges with MFA, as the email verification will go to the shared inbox. To avoid issues, it’s recommended to use individual, unique email addresses for each user. If it is not possible to use individual accounts, please make sure you have access to the shared email account, as the MFA process will be done in that same email address.
